(1997-Present)

Source: San Francisco Open Data - datasf.org

Color Code: Green - 'Dot Com' Era, Red - Great Recession, Yellow - All other years

Owner Move-in is the most common reason for evcition notice in San Francisco. While not all owner move-in evictions are disingenuous, it is a well established fact that many are fraudulent, and an easy way to kick out rent controlled tenants. Tenants face the option of paying the high cost for a laywer to fight the eviction and potentially having an eviction on their record, making it nearly impossible to rent elsewhere in the city, so many just accept and move on.
